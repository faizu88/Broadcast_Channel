Broadcast Channel Steps:

  1. Open a new 'Tab' or 'Window' by copying or duplicating the current tab URL (Say 'Tab A' [Current Tab], 'Tab B' [New tab])
  2. Click the button 'CREATE/JOIN CHANNEL' on both 'Tab A' and 'Tab B'
  3. In 'Tab A' type anything inside the textarea
  4. Click the button 'SEND MESSAGE' on 'Tab A'
  5. Now open the 'Tab B', you'll see the received message
